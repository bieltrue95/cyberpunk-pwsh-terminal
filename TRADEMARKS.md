# Trademark Usage Policy

This project may reference third-party product names, services, and trademarks (for example, "Windows Terminal", "PowerShell", "GitHub", "Nerd Fonts", and "oh-my-posh").

## Rules

1. References to third-party marks are descriptive only (compatibility, integration, or instructions).
2. References must not imply sponsorship, endorsement, partnership, or affiliation.
3. Do not use third-party logos or brand assets in this repository unless license/permission is documented.
4. When possible, prefer wording such as "compatible with" instead of ownership-like language.
5. If a trademark owner requests adjustment or removal, follow the takedown process in `LEGAL_COMPLIANCE.md`.

## Ownership

All third-party trademarks remain the property of their respective owners.
