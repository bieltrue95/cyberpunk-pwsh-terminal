## Policy

1. New media assets (PNG, JPG, GIF, WebP, MP4, MP3, fonts, binaries) must be listed here in the same PR.
2. Every listed asset must include Source and License.
3. If source/license cannot be proven, the asset must be removed or replaced before merge.
