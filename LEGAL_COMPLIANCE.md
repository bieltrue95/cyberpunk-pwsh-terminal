# Legal Compliance Policy

This document defines minimum legal/compliance rules for this open source repository.

## Contribution Policy

Contributors must submit only:

- Original content created by the contributor, or
- Third-party content with a compatible license and required attribution.

Contributors must not submit:

- Proprietary content from employers/clients without written authorization.
- Confidential material, private data, credentials, tokens, or private keys.
- Third-party assets without clear source/license information.

## Legal Notice

- This repository and documentation are provided for technical reference.
- Nothing in this repository is legal advice.
- Use is at your own risk, consistent with the `LICENSE` disclaimer.

## Jurisdiction and Takedown Contact

- Preferred jurisdiction for repository governance discussions: Brazil.
- Takedown and legal notices contact: open a private security report (see `SECURITY.md`) or contact the maintainer through the repository owner profile.

## Takedown / Notice Response Process

1. Acknowledge notice within 48 hours.
2. Triage claim scope (file(s), commit(s), dependency/asset/license basis).
3. If plausible, temporarily remove or restrict disputed material pending review.
4. Audit repository history and current branch for related occurrences.
5. Publish remediation commit(s) and update legal documentation.
6. Respond formally with actions taken and residual risk status.
